Doraemon is a Japanese manga and anime series created by Fujiko F. Fujio.01 It tells the story of a male robotic earless cat named Nobita who travels back in time from the 22nd century to aid a preteen boy named Nobita.The two form a close bond and every episode sees Nobita with a new problem and Doraemon with a new gadget to help him solve it. The manga was first serialized in December 1969, with its 1,345 individual chapters compiled into 45 tankbon volumes and published by Shogakukan from 1970 to 1996.
