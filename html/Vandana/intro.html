What is Social media ?...

Social media means websites and applications that enable users to create and share content or to participate in social networking. Platforms like Facebook, Instagram and Twitter are some of the examples of social media.

What is the use of social media?

Social media is about conversations, community, connecting with the audience and building relationships. It is not just a broadcast channel or a sales and marketing tool. Authenticity, honesty and open dialogue are key. Social media not only allows you to hear what people say about you, but enables you to respond.

What are the five main benefits of social media?

1.Stay updated with all the new things in the world. 2.Communicate anytime, anywhere from the comfort of your home. Advertising platforms for brands to find their right-set of consumers. Easy to build relationships and connecting with like-minded people.

What age is OK for social media?

Social Media and Kids: Making an Informed Family Decision. Considering safety and privacy, it's not advised that young people under the age of 13 be on social media. In fact, most social platforms require youth to be age 13 or older to sign up.
