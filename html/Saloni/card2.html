vikram singh

Vikram Singh Chauhan is an Indian actor who mainly works in Hindi television. He made his acting debut with Qubool Hai portraying Imran Qureshi in 2013. He is best known for his portrayal of Atharv Vashisht in Jaana Na Dil Se Door, Vyom Bedi in Ek Deewaana Tha and Aman Khan in Yehh Jadu Hai Jinn Ka!.
