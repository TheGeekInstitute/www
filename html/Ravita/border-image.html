Radha Rani is the eternal consort and beloved of Lord Krishna, the supreme personality of Godhead. She is the embodiment of divine love, bliss, and devotion, and the source of all the goddesses of fortune.She appeared on earth as the daughter of King Vrishabhanu and Queen Kirtida in the village of Barsana, near Vrindavan.She enchanted Krishna with her beauty, charm, and sweetness, and they performed many pastimes together in the forests and groves of Vrindavan.She enchanted Krishna with her beauty, charm, and sweetness, and they performed many pastimes together in the forests and groves of Vrindavan.
