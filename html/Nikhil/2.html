How To Login Instagram

Enter your Instagram username or phone number,
then tap Send login link.
If you've previously linked your Instagram and Facebook accounts,
you can reset your password using your Facebook account by tapping Reset using Facebook.

How to logout instagram

log out of your Instagram account on a Windows, Mac,
Linux, or Chromebook computer,
blaunch the Instagram site in a web browser on your computer.
On the Instagram site, in the top-right corner, click your profile icon.
From the menu that opens after clicking the profile icon, select “Log Out.”
